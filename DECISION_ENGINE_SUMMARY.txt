   POST /api/decision-engine
   {"action": "explain", "offerId": "offer_123"}

   POST /api/decision-engine
   {"action": "segments"}
   ↓

4️⃣  Run Autonomous Optimization
   POST /api/decision-engine
   {"action": "autonomy"}
   ↓
   Returns: # offers auto-paused, # discounts tuned, # placements reallocated

5️⃣  View Dashboard
   GET /app/explainability
   ↓
   Shows: Segment performance, context injection, automation journey, guardrails
